How do I create a new account?

üìÉ Park Reservation

How do I reserve a parking slot?
  • Log in and click the "Reserve Now" button on the homepage.
  • Select the date, parking spot, and available time slot.
  • Click "Reserve" to proceed to the reservation details page.
  • Enter your car details, then click "Continue".
How can I manage my reservations?

Go to the "My Account" page, click "Reservations" to view and manage your bookings.
